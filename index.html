    ACM VIT is one of the most popular chapters in VIT. ACM VIT student chapter was started up in October 2009 by Kuldeep Singh. Since then we at the ACM VIT student chapter have carried on the legacy of ACM international. The ACM International is a learned society for computing. ACM is an educational and scientific society which works with the motto of "Advancing Computing as a Science and Profession".
